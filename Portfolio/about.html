About Me

"Every great dream, begins with a dreamer. Always remember, you have within you the strength, the patience, and the passion to reach for the stars to change the world.” -Harriet Tubman

I was working on a project in middle school when I came across this quote. It has stuck with me ever since, the knowledge that with patience and passion, change can happen. I have the passion for creativity: reading, writing, drawing, and especially coding, for the past fourteen years. I have expanded on it in ways I never knew possible, ways I didn’t dream of when I was a kid. But I am grateful, every day, that I have kept it close to my heart.

I am an aspiring full-stacked developer who has worked with a pleathora of programs over the years to create web and graphic designs. Coding has been part of my life for many years. I created my first website as a child at my father's job on a "take your child to work" day. I continued that passion, when I met a group of fantastic online friends in gaming, and have continued with it till now. As passionate as I am about art work from drawing and writing, web design and developing seems to be an excellent way to bring it all together. I can only hope to expand on this, practicing and learning to keep things simple and inspiring others in the process.

There is a lot to learn and I have every intention of continuing to learn as technology develops. I hope with this knowledge, I will be able to join a team and work on projects, and even create new projects in my personal time for others who may need it.

My passion and pride drive creativeness, while also keeping cleanliness, modern and unique approaches to design will make your site one that people enjoy coming back to.
